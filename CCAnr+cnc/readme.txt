For more details about CCAnr+cnc, please refer to 'src/readme.txt'

Command string for calling CCAnr+cnc
./CCAnr+cnc.sh <instance> <seed> <cutoff_time>

Example:
./CCAnr+cnc.sh Ptn-7824-b21.cnf 1 10
means that CCAnr+cnc solves the instance 'Ptn-7824-b21.cnf' with the random seed of 1 and the cutoff time of 10 seconds.
